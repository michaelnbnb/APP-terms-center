MediMinder Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

MediMinder is designed with your privacy in mind. We only collect and store the information that's necessary to provide you with medication reminder services:

  • Medication details (names, schedules, dosages)
  • Reminder preferences
  • App usage statistics for improving our service

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Providing medication reminders
  • Improving app functionality
  • Technical support when you contact us

Data Storage

All your medication data is stored securely and locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. This service is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
